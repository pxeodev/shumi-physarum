# The Shumi Swarm

![Shumi Swarm](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/haddencarpenter/shumi-physarum/main/og-thumbnail.png)

**Design how your Shumi agent sees the world.**

The Shumi Swarm Generator is the visual identity system for the upcoming limited-edition Shumi agents. Every Shumi agent carries a unique swarm pattern — a living, breathing network that represents its neural pathways and decision-making topology.

This is where you choose yours.

## What is a Swarm Pattern?

Each pattern is generated by thousands of autonomous cells that explore outward from a central intelligence hub, depositing signal trails that self-organize into emergent networks. No two seeds produce the same result. The patterns are deterministic — your seed, your swarm, every time.

Think of it as your agent's fingerprint: a visual signature of how it processes, connects, and navigates.

## What You Can Do

- **Generate unique swarm patterns** from any seed number
- **Customize every parameter** — density, speed, branching, signal strength
- **Choose from 8 swarm palettes** or create your own color identity
- **Export as PNG, WebM video, or animated GIF** for avatars and profiles
- **Preview and batch-record** multiple seeds in one session
- **Fullscreen 1080p capture** for hero backgrounds and presentations

## Quick Start

1. Open the generator in any modern browser (Chrome recommended)
2. The swarm starts growing automatically with seed `12345`
3. Click **Random** to discover new patterns
4. Adjust the sidebar sliders to shape the swarm's behavior
5. Hit **Save PNG** or **Record WebM** to capture your pattern

No installation. No build step. No dependencies. Just open and explore.
