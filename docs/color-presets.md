# Swarm Palettes

Every Shumi agent's visual identity is defined by three colors. The generator includes 8 curated palettes — or create your own.

## How Swarm Colors Work

Every pixel on the canvas uses a three-stop color system:

1. **Signal Base** — The darkest tone, applied to faint trails and quiet zones
2. **Signal Bright** — The primary network color, applied to well-traveled paths
3. **Hub Accent** — Blended into the central hub and highest-intensity areas

The signal intensity (0–1) determines which color you see. Distance from the intelligence hub adds warmth via the accent color.

## Custom Palettes

Click any of the three color pickers to define a unique color identity. Changes apply instantly.

**Tips for strong custom palettes:**
- Keep **Signal Base** very dark (near black) — it forms the void between trails
- **Signal Bright** is the star — pick the most saturated, distinctive color
- **Hub Accent** appears only at the center and peak intensity — use it as a contrast pop
- High contrast between Base and Bright produces the most legible networks
